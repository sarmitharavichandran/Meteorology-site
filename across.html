OVERALL AIR QUALITY OVER DELHI IS IN "POOR" CATEGORY. UNDER THE INFLUENCE TWO SUCCESSIVE WESTERN DISTURBANCE, THE REGION IS COVERED BY SHALLOW CLOUDS WITHOUT MUCH RAIN. THE CLOUDY SKY HAS PREVENTED THE BOUNDARY LAYER EVOLUTION AND HENCE POLLUTANT DISPERSION. ALSO EXPECTED RAIN AND WET DEPOSITION HAS NOT OCCURRED THIS HAS LED TO DETERIORATION OF AIR QUALITY INSTEAD OF IMPROVEMENT. THE AIR QUALITY IS EXPECTED TO SLIGHTLY IMPROVE AND REGISTER IN MODERATE TO POOR CATEGORY FOR TOMORROW. NO FURTHER DETERIORATION IS EXPECTED FOR NEXT THREE DAYS.
